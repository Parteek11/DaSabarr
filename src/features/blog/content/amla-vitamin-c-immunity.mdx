Amla, or Indian gooseberry, contains up to 20 times more Vitamin C than an orange by weight. But its benefits extend far beyond this single nutrient.

## A Complete Wellness Package

Amla is rich in:

- **Vitamin C** — essential for immune function and collagen synthesis
- **Bioflavonoids** — enhance Vitamin C absorption and provide antioxidant protection
- **Tannins** — support digestive health and nutrient absorption

## Traditional Uses

In Ayurveda, amla is classified as a *rasayana* — a rejuvenating herb that promotes longevity, strengthens immunity, and supports healthy aging. It's a key ingredient in Chyawanprash, one of the oldest documented herbal formulations.

## Modern Applications

Today, amla powder is used in:

- Daily immunity tonics
- Hair and skin care formulations
- Smoothie and juice blends
- Capsule supplements

Not all amla powders are created equal. Look for products that use sun-dried (not heat-processed) fruits to preserve the delicate Vitamin C content. Da Sabarr's amla powder undergoes rigorous testing to ensure maximum potency.
